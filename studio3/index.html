There are 5 layers in total, though for this quiz we're only going to be examining the three layers closest to the surface: the Epipelagic (Sunlight) Zone, Mesopelagic (Twilight) Zone, and Bathypelagic (Midnight) Zone. For each animal on this quiz, you'll have to guess which ocean layer they live in!
